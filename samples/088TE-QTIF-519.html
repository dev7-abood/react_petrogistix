QTIF-519 WILL BE DRILLED AND COMPLETED FROM DRILL SITE NWD-1 AS AN OPEN HOLE SINGLE LATERAL POWER WATER INJECTOR TARGETING ARAB-C RESERVOIR.
WELL TD WILL BE 20,738 FT MD / 7,576 FT TVD. THE WELL WILL BE COMPLETED WITH 7" GRE TUBING (NON-CEMENTED TIE-BACK TO SURFACE).
THIS DESIGN IS TRIALED TO ACCOMMODATE HIGHER INJECTION RATES AS PER RMD SIMULATION. TO ACCOMDATE FOR THIS DESIGN CHANGE, A MODIFIED BIG HOLE DESIGN IS PLANNED.
THE WELL WILL START WITH 42" HOLE SIZE. ALTHOUGH CASING POINTS WILL NOT BE DIFFERENT, CASING SIZES WILL CHANGE. THE STARTER HEAD (CHH) WILL BE 20-3/4" X 18-5/8".
THE MAIN REASON FOR THESE MODIFICATIONS IS TO COVER WASIA WITH TWO CEMENTED CASING STRINGS, MAINLY 13-3/8" AND 9-5/8".
============================================ ERP & SAFETY REQUIREMENT =================================
THE WELL IS PLANNED AS ZERO RER INJECTOR WELL DESIGNED TO PENETRATE WATER ZONES ONLY.
IF ANY MOVABLE HYDROCARBONS ARE INTERSECTED WHILE DRILLING QTIF-519, DRILLING MUST BE STOPPED.
THE WELL SHALL BE CEMENTED ACROSS THE HYDROCARBON ZONE AND SIDE-TRACKED DOWN-DIP OF THE HYDROCARBON ZONE.
THE SITUATION MUST BE REGISTERED AND NOTIFIED TO DHALPD. MINIMUM SPACING IS 60 M.
